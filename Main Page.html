Overview

Warhammer 40k is a sci-fi universe that inspired the grimdark genre, a genre that depicts a universe with no hope. These worlds are filled with countleess heroic deeds and many examples of bravery that defy logic but are ultimately meaningless in the face of incoming doom only able to hold the tide of evil but never able to push it back..

This universe is filled with many races and many ideologies, but can be distinguished into 3 groups by the in lore humans. The first are the humans who I will be discussing and all of the factions within, the second faction are the xenos who are the many alien groups all put into one pot (Orkz, Eldar, Tau, Necrons, Tyranids), and finaly there is the last group the daemons who are all of the creatures not of this reality.
