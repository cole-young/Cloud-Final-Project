Adeptus Sororitas

A Brief Rundown.

Home Astartes Mechanicus Guard

Overview

The adeptis Sororatas serve the ecclesiarchy or the church of the imperium of man as an all female fighting force. Lacking in the genetic modifications of the astartes or the numbers of the guard or the technology of the mechanicus the sororitas rely on religeous fervor instead, able to chanel their holy rage into an actual physical effect surviving mortal wounds and performing feats akin to miracles. Decked out in power armor and armed with boltguns both of which are not as powerful as the astartes version these sisters of battle march out with undying faith ready to purge all that stand in the emperors path.

The ecclesiarchy used to own its own standing army after the space marines were seperated into chapters but soon after they also revolted against the imperium and threw it into chaos. After their defeat by the imperium it was agreed that the ecclesiarchy would no longer be allowed to have "men at arms". So of course directly afterwards the church created the Adeptis Sororitas.
